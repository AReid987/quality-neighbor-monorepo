# Product Mission (Lite)

Quality Neighbor is a hyperlocal community platform that helps residents and local businesses in neighborhoods like Hartland Ranch connect authentically by providing a trusted, curated, and positive alternative to noisy social media feeds.

Quality Neighbor serves community residents feeling disconnected and local businesses seeking effective advertising. Unlike algorithm-driven social media, we provide a professionally curated, positive-only newsletter and platform with agent-assisted ad management to ensure high-quality, relevant content and a trusted environment.
